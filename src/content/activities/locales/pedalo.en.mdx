Set out for a ride on Lake Saint-Ferréol by pedal boat. Perfect for families and groups of friends, this activity lets you enjoy the lake's exceptional natural setting while having fun.

## What you need to know

- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Capacity**: 4 to 5 people per pedal boat
- **Level**: Suitable for all, from age 3 with an adult
- **Equipment**: Life jackets provided

## Good to know

Lake Saint-Ferréol provides a magnificent setting for this activity. You can admire the historic dam, wooded banks and perhaps spot some ducks along the way.
